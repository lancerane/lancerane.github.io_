Our technology

We are building the next generation of wearable rehabilitation tech for athletes post-injury and patients with osteoarthritis. Our patented technology, packed into a pair of shorts, modulates muscle activity in real time in response to the wearer's movements.

AI on board

Our algorithms read a continuous stream of motion data up to 1000 times per second

Muscle stimulation

Movement optimisation is achieved by electrical stimulation of muscles in real time

Recovery tracking

Movement data can be used to track recovery progress
