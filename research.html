Research

Making AI portable

Machine learning models typically have prohibitive energy and compute requirements. We are developing novel efficiency techniques to enable usage on portable, battery-powered hardware.

Musculoskeletal simulation

Taking patient data measured on the field, we create computer models of movement, allowing us to simulate the effects of altered muscle firing patterns.

ACL injury in elite athletes

Using FES to enhance rehabilitation following sports injury. Electrical current delivers a direct (muscular) and an indirect (sensory) training stimulus.

Kinematics as biomarker in osteoarthritis

Characterising movement deficits in OA as measured using tiny body-worn sensors.
